About Owen

Graphic Designer and Illustrator

Owen is an inventive graphic designer and illustrator from Cincinnati, Ohio. He is a student attending Ohio University and is studying publication design in the Scripps School of Visual Communication.

At a young age, Owen discovered his love for photography and experimented a lot in photoshop with surrealist photo manipulations. His interest in photography led him into the world of graphic design and illustration, which opened up a door full of opportunities. He was drawn to the creative freedom and individuality that design and illustration offers, and chose to pursue a career in this field.

Owen will tackle any type of project thrown at him, however, his passion lies within the music and entertainment industry. His goal is to progress as an illustrator and create event posters, album covers, animations, etc. for the creative entertainment industry.
